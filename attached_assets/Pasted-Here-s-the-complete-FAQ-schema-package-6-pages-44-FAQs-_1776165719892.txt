Why This Maximizes AI Citations
FAQPage for LLM extraction
Google killed FAQPage rich results for non-gov sites in 2024 — but AI Overviews, Perplexity, and ChatGPT still parse FAQPage schema as structured Q&A blocks. Every question is written in natural query format matching how users actually ask LLMs (e.g., "What is a pig butchering scam in crypto?").

Wikidata entity linking
